About Me

Hello! My name is Cassidy Belk, and I am a junior marketing major at Valparaiso University in Valparaiso, Indiana. I grew up in Northwest Indiana as the oldest of five children. When I was a kid, I passed the time by playing softball, painting, and learning to play various musical instruments, a few of which I still do as an adult. I have a passion for music, and my favorite instruments to play are guitar, bass, piano, ukulele, and drums. Because of my love for music, I listen to it constantly. My favorite music genres are rock, metal, alternative, and R&B. At Valparaiso University, I have put my musical interests to work for Hooked On Tonics A capella, which I am the president of.

In addition to my noteable love for music, I have many other demonstrated interests and hobbies. I have a soft spot for animals, especially my recently rescued puppy named Chuck Norris: a border collie yorkie mix. Some of my other interests include art, graphic design, motorcycles, and baking. I have varied interests that stretch across a wide range of industries.

When I am more developed in my career, I hope to travel the world and see as much of it as I can. I enjoy meeting new people and hearing their unique stories. In the summer of 2019, I discovered my love for traveling when I spent three months on the west coast near San Fransisco, California.

I consider myself a very strong-willed person. Despite my dedicated passion for certain matters, I try to be mindful of opinions different from my own. Some of my personal values include honesty, integrity, empathy, and responsibility. In my everyday life, I like to research and actively engage in political activism for the movements I am passionate about such as Black Lives Matter, LGBTQ+ rights, women's rights, etc. My interest in activism and learning about others led me to Sigma Lambda Gamma National Sorority. Becoming a Gamma and being surrounded by so many inspirational women has helped me find the balance between being strong and outspoken but also understanding and kind. Regardless of my own opinions, I try my best to make every decision, no matter how big or small, with the feelings and wellbeing of others in mind.
